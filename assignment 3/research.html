Research

Machine Learning-Assisted Measurements, specifically:

  • Fundamentals of Machine Learning in Measurement: VIM/GUM-compliant Uncertainty Quantification for Regression and Classification, Measurement Standards.
  • Vision Based Measurement: Human Activity Recognition, Driver Yawning Detection, Food Computing: Image-Based Calorie Measurement, Eating/Cooking Activity Recognition.
  • Multimedia Systems and Network Measurements: Autonomous Network Fault Diagnosis, Action Recommendation for Network Operation Centers, Network Delay Prediction, Bandwidth Prediction in Video Networks, Player Engagement Estimation in Games, Serious Games for Medical Diagnosis: ADHD and VSMD Detection.
  • IoT Measurements: Machinery Fault Detection, Operation Status Tracking, IoT Data Acquisition and Transport.
